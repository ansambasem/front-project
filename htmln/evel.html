Information about the most important landmarks of Paris

The Eiffel Tower is one of the most famous landmarks in Europe and the world. It is located in Paris, in the northwestern area of the Champ de Mars park, at the far end of the French capital, very close to the Seine River. It is considered one of the Seven Wonders of the World and is highly renowned.

The Eiffel Tower is the most significant tourist attraction in France, generating millions of euros annually as it draws visitors from all over the globe. Since its construction in 1889 until 1930, it held the title of the tallest building in the world for 41 years. The construction of the tower took two years, and it was inaugurated on March 31, 1889.

Primarily, the Eiffel Tower is used as an observation tower and a broadcasting tower for radio and television. It remains an iconic symbol of innovation and architecture, attracting millions of tourists each year to experience its breathtaking views of Paris from its elevated platforms.
