Information about the most important landmarks of Dubai

Burj Khalifa: Brief and Important Information

  • Location: Located in Dubai, United Arab Emirates.
  • Opening: Opened on January 4, 2010.
  • Height: Stands at 828 meters, making it the tallest building in the world.
  • Floors: It has 163 floors above ground.
  • Usage: It includes residential apartments, offices, the luxurious Armani Hotel, and restaurants.
  • Records: Holds several world records, including the highest observation deck, the fastest elevator, and the tallest elevator in the world.
  • Design: Inspired by the Hymenocallis flower, it features modern Islamic architecture.
  • Construction: Construction began in 2004 and took 6 years, costing about $1.5 billion.
  • Tourist Attraction: It houses the "At The Top" observation deck, offering stunning views of Dubai and the Arabian Gulf.
